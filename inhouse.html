Inhouse

A broad repertoire tailored to suit diverse audiences

Overview

Inhouse is a dynamic variety band that has quickly risen to popularity across Siquijor, Siquijor. Fusing youthful stage presence with a competitive edge in live performance, they’ve become one of the island’s most sought-after music acts. From lively town events to private parties and resort gigs, Inhouse brings a refreshing mix of energy, versatility, and charm to every show.

What makes Inhouse stand out is their ability to connect with audiences across all ages. Their playlist spans a wide range—from current pop hits and dance anthems to classic OPM and timeless ballads—executed with skill and flair. Every performance is charged with vibrant vocals, tight instrumentals, and that special spark that keeps the crowd moving and wanting more.

Inhouse doesn't just play music—they bring life to the party. Their youthful vibe translates into contagious excitement, yet they perform with the discipline and professionalism of seasoned musicians. Whether playing at a beachfront gig, corporate function, or local celebration, the band adapts seamlessly to the vibe and ensures a lively, crowd-pleasing show. For event organizers looking for a fresh, reliable, and crowd-favorite band, Inhouse delivers entertainment that’s fun, memorable, and full of heart.

Brief History

Formed in 2023, Inhouse emerged as a fresh yet seasoned force in the local music scene, composed of core members from the once-popular party band La Isla Vibes, which had been a staple in island nightlife for years. Their formation marked the revival of musical energy among fans who had followed La Isla Vibes through countless high-energy gigs and festival performances.

The name Inhouse reflects their collaborative spirit and shared history—rooted in tight musical chemistry, homegrown talent, and a deep connection to the island's evolving soundscape. Though newly formed, the band carries with it years of experience, as its members are no strangers to large crowds, diverse genres, and the demands of live entertainment.

Drawing from their party band legacy, Inhouse seamlessly blends familiarity with a new creative direction. Their performances showcase a mix of nostalgia and innovation, breathing new life into the local music scene and re-engaging audiences with a more intimate, refined vibe.

Inhouse continues to redefine what post-pandemic island music can be—resilient, united, and always ready to bring the house down.

Styles

Musically, Inhouse brings a sound rooted in party pop—vibrant, feel-good, and built to get crowds moving. Their performances are charged with infectious energy, blending catchy hooks, smooth transitions, and dynamic stage presence that turns any event into a celebration. While party music is their core, the band’s true strength lies in their musical versatility.

Drawing from their experience with La Isla Vibes, the members of Inhouse are no strangers to a wide range of styles. Whether it’s acoustic, reggae, OPM ballads, R&B, or even funky medleys, they perform with precision and flair. Their ability to shift between genres keeps their setlists fresh and engaging—perfect for any type of audience or venue.

Every Inhouse performance is tailored to the moment. They know how to read a crowd, switching effortlessly from an upbeat pop anthem to a slow groove or sing-along classic. Their sets often include unexpected mashups and spontaneous arrangements, showing off not just their musical chops but their creativity and chemistry as a band.

Inhouse doesn’t just play music—they bring an experience. With tight instrumentation, polished vocals, and a contagious energy, they turn familiar songs into something memorable and uniquely their own, keeping audiences coming back for more.

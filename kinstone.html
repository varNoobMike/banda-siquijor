Kinstone

Island-inspired reggae rhythms with oceanic soul

Overview

Kinstone Reggae is another incredible reggae band in Siquijor, celebrated for their vibrant sound and heartfelt performances. As one of the island’s standout musical acts, they bring a fresh yet authentic take on the reggae genre, earning admiration from both locals and visitors alike. With every show, Kinstone Reggae transforms the stage into a lively celebration of rhythm, unity, and good vibes.

Known for their dynamic energy and smooth musical flow, Kinstone Reggae captivates audiences with performances that balance technical skill and emotional depth. Whether playing in intimate beach settings or large community events, they create a magnetic connection with the crowd, leaving lasting impressions through their music.

More than just performers, Kinstone Reggae represents the soul of Siquijor’s island culture—warm, soulful, and full of life. Their musical journey reflects a deep-rooted love for reggae, along with a commitment to sharing messages of peace, positivity, and resilience through every beat and lyric.

Brief History

Kinstone is one of the early reggae bands in Siquijor, older than SoulSight and instrumental in shaping the island’s reggae scene. Formed by passionate musicians with a deep love for roots reggae, they quickly gained recognition for their authentic island grooves and positive messages.

Starting out in the early days of Siquijor’s live music scene, Kinstone played at town fiestas, beachside gatherings, and grassroots events that helped define the island’s musical culture. Their timeless sound and strong community connection earned them lasting respect from fans and fellow artists alike.

Today, as veterans of Siquijor’s reggae movement, Kinstone Reggae continues to inspire new generations of musicians. Their decades-long dedication stands as a foundation of the island’s reggae legacy, keeping the spirit of the genre alive and thriving.

Styles

Kinstone Reggae’s sound is rooted in classic reggae, with a strong emphasis on rhythm, harmony, and conscious lyrics. From their early days, the band has embraced reggae’s soulful essence, delivering music that speaks of island life, peace, love, and social awareness—resonating deeply with both local and visiting audiences.

As one of the pioneers of the genre in Siquijor, Kinstone also explores various reggae sub-genres, including roots, rockers, and dub. Their music often features smooth grooves, rich harmonies, and layered textures that showcase their technical skill and deep understanding of reggae’s many forms.

While staying true to traditional reggae foundations, Kinstone infuses their performances with subtle influences from ska and rocksteady, creating a timeless and well-rounded sound. This stylistic versatility keeps their sets fresh and engaging, cementing their place as one of the most respected and enduring reggae acts on the island.

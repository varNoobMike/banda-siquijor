About Banda Siquijor

Banda Siquijor is a website created to promote the finest bands in Siquijor. It highlights local musical talent and provides an online space where bands from the island can be seen and heard by a wider audience.

The website features band profiles, event schedules, and performance highlights to help connect artists with fans, organizers, and the broader music community. Banda Siquijor supports and celebrates the island's growing music scene by giving local bands the exposure they deserve.

Mission

This website exists to promote and elevate the exposure of local bands in Siquijor. The site aims to bring attention to the island’s talented musicians and help connect them with a wider audience.

Uplift Local Music

Supporting local bands in Siquijor is more than just enjoying good music — it's about helping a vibrant community of artists grow, thrive, and share their talents with the world. Your small actions can make a big difference in the lives of these musicians and the island's music culture as a whole. Here are a few simple ways you can show your support:

  • Attend live gigs, events, and local music festivals to show your presence and appreciation
  • Tip musicians during performances as a token of gratitude for their art and effort
  • Share photos, videos, and experiences from gigs on social media to help promote local talent
  • Invite friends and visitors to experience the local music scene firsthand
  • Encourage venues and organizers to book local bands for community events
